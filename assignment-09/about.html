Location

Grizzly Gardens rests within Grizzly Gulch, south of Helena, and is surrounded by beautiful mountains and forests, resting against the Helena National Forest. Above 5 miles into the Gulch, enjoy the peace and natural wonder of the rustic western town built into the sloping hills of the mountains.




Events

Enjoy more than just weddings. The venue is available for any event you wish to host, including reunions, baby showers, photography sessions, conferences, and much more.
